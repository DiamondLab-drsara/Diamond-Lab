Understanding Your Diamond

Natural vs Lab-Grown

طبيعي VS مخبري

Lab-grown diamonds share the same atomic structure, refractive index, and hardness (10 on Mohs) as natural diamonds. The only difference is origin — and price.

Diamond vs Moissanite

الماس VS موزنايت

Moissanite is silicon carbide; a diamond simulant with higher fire but lower hardness (9.25). Our lab-grown diamonds are real diamonds — certified and permanent.

The Perfect Solitaire

سوليتير

A solitaire setting places one magnificent stone at the center of all attention. Clean, timeless, and endlessly symbolic — it lets the diamond speak for itself.
